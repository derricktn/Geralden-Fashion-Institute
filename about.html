A Message From Our Founder

Welcome to Geralden Fashion Institute, where dreams are woven into reality and creativity knows no bounds. For over two decades, I've dedicated my life to the art of fashion, and it is with immense pride that I welcome you to our institution.

Our journey began in a small shop with just three sewing machines and a vision to transform fashion education. Today, we stand as a beacon of excellence, having nurtured thousands of talented designers who are now shaping the global fashion landscape.

At Geralden Fashion Institute, we believe that true fashion education goes beyond technical skills. It's about cultivating a unique creative voice, understanding the cultural significance of clothing, and developing the business acumen to thrive in this dynamic industry.

Evalove Andoh
